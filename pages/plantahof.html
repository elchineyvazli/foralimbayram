PRECEDENT STUDY OF THE PLANTAHOF AUDITORIUM

As part of a group project involving a precedent analysis of an existing building in Landquart, Switzerland, designed by Valerio Olgiati, I was tasked with creating a 1:2 scale physical model of the structure. To achieve this, I utilized Rockite Anchoring Cement, a fast-drying material that allowed me to create a highly accurate and detailed replica of the building. The model was carefully constructed over a period of several hours, with close attention paid to the precise placement and alignment of each element to ensure an accurate representation of the original structure.
